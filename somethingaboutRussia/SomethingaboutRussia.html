Something about Russia (according to the russian people)

There are many people all over the world who might heard something about that misterious country. There are a huge amount of stereotypes about russians, but now we are going to dispel (or maybe confirm) some of these myths.

1) Russians don't show their emotions

Well, some foreigners, who were in Russia for the first time, usually tell that russians are really gloomy. And this is part of the truth. Even I can be really gloomy sometimes as I'm a russian, but that doesn't matter that we aren't kind. If you ask russian for help, he or she will surely help you.

2) Russian language sounds rude

Yes, our language is amazing and really unusual for foreigner's hearing, but many people from other countries are fond of russian music with lyrics, they also find such pronounciation attractive.

3) Russian food is really strange

Even I find our meals unusual, but it's easy to cook. For example, different salads and cheesecakes (fried cottage cheese). Of course, you need to learn how to cook so that it was delicious but it's worth it!

So that was three major stereotypes about russians but I think, the best way to check them out is to see everything with your own eyes!
